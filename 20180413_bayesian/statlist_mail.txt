We are happy to announce our 3rd R-Ladies Lausanne Meetup. 


"R for Bayesian statistics analyses"
 
Presenter: Elizaveta Semenova from Swiss Tropical and Public Health Institute, Basel (https://elizavetasemenova.github.io/)

Date: April 13 at noon (lunch included)
Location: Swisscom Digital Lab (EPFL - Innovation Park)

Prerequisite:  
A basic knowledge or classical (frequentist) statistics is needed.
Ideally, bring a laptop with installed R and the RStan library:
- Linux: https://github.com/stan-dev/rstan/wiki/Installing-RStan-on-Mac-or-Linux
- Windows: https://github.com/stan-dev/rstan/wiki/Installing-RStan-on-Windows

Registration is free but mandatory:
https://www.meetup.com/fr-FR/rladies-lausanne/events/248694983/
